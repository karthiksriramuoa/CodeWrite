Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need a website?

We hope you know the answer to this one yourself! It’s an important question as it is the fundamental basis for developing the website. Are you trying to: sell a product? sell a service? promote an event? build awareness? A clear understanding of what you want your new website to achieve is needed, along with a sound strategy. When we understand these things we can develop a clear site plan that will provide the user with the information they need to buy from you, make an enquiry or booking.

How long will it take to setup my website?

There honestly isn’t any hard and fast rule here. It depends on the complexity of the project, whether it is a templated or fully custom designed website, and our workload at the time. Even how quickly you can make decisions will affect things! We have had sites up and running the same week, while other projects can run for six months before the site goes live. We can give you an expected ‘go live’ date when we have met with you and scoped out the amount of work required.

How will my site look on mobile devices?

It depends very much on how the site is designed. On devices down to tablet size every website should look fine. When you get to mobile phone size, many websites don’t appear great due to the very small screen size. Some web templates are designed to be ‘responsive’, meaning they automatically rearrange the layout to in response to the screen size of the device. These are becoming more common and are a great option if you want to future proof your site, or think you may have many people browsing using their phones.
